About Me

Since I was a kid, my curiosity has spanned technology, history, music, arts, and knowledge in general. I've always had an affinity for electronics and, by age 5, I was already exploring my father's VCR.

My first contact with a computer was in 2003, at an internet café (known as a "Lan House" in Brazil). In 2006, I got my first PC and was fascinated, determined to explore everything it could do. That's where my passion for Computer Science was born.

After school, I followed another great passion and, with a scholarship, I studied History. Currently, I am in a career transition and have returned to my roots: I am learning to program with the renowned CS50 course and dedicating myself to the study of Computer Science.

My Hobbies and Focus

Music: I'm a guitarist. Martial Arts: I practice Karate (I'm a blue belt). Technology and Movies.

My focus is to enter the tech industry as a programmer, and I believe I am on the right path with CS50.
